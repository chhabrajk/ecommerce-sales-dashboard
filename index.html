What This Dashboard Covers

📈 Monthly Revenue Trend
Area chart showing revenue momentum month-over-month across the full 2-year period with seasonal patterns highlighted.
🍕 Category Breakdown
Interactive pie chart showing revenue share across 6 product categories — drill down by applying year and region filters.
🗺️ Regional Performance
Horizontal bar chart ranking all 5 regions by revenue — instantly spot underperforming markets vs top performers.
📋 Order Status Mix
Bar chart tracking Delivered, Shipped, Cancelled, and Returned orders — monitor fulfilment health at a glance.
🔢 Live KPI Cards
5 KPI cards update in real time as filters change — Total Revenue, Orders, AOV, Cancellation Rate, Unique Customers.
⚡ Built with Plotly Dash
Full Python web app — run locally for the complete interactive experience with all dropdowns and live callbacks.
